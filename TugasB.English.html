Rumus dan Contoh Semua Tenses

1. Simple Present Tense

  • Subject + V1 (s/es untuk he, she, it)
  • Subject + do/does + not + V1
  • Do/Does + Subject + V1?
She reads a book.
He does not play football.
Do you like coffee?

2. Present Continuous Tense

  • Subject + is/am/are + V-ing
  • Subject + is/am/are + not + V-ing
  • Is/Am/Are + Subject + V-ing?
I am studying English.
They are not watching TV.
Are you listening to music?

3. Present Perfect Tense

  • Subject + has/have + V3
  • Subject + has/have + not + V3
  • Has/Have + Subject + V3?
She has finished her homework.
I have not seen that movie.
Have you eaten breakfast?

4. Present Perfect Continuous Tense

  • Subject + has/have + been + V-ing
  • Subject + has/have + not + been + V-ing
  • Has/Have + Subject + been + V-ing?
He has been working all day.
They have not been sleeping.
Have you been studying?

5. Simple Past Tense

  • Subject + V2
  • Subject + did not + V1
  • Did + Subject + V1?
She visited her grandma.
He did not go to school.
Did you watch the movie?

6. Past Continuous Tense

  • Subject + was/were + V-ing
  • Subject + was/were + not + V-ing
  • Was/Were + Subject + V-ing?
I was reading a book.
They were not playing outside.
Were you sleeping?

7. Past Perfect Tense

  • Subject + had + V3
  • Subject + had not + V3
  • Had + Subject + V3?
She had left before I arrived.
He had not eaten dinner.
Had you seen him before?

8. Past Perfect Continuous Tense

  • Subject + had been + V-ing
  • Subject + had not been + V-ing
  • Had + Subject + been + V-ing?
They had been studying all night.
I had not been working.
Had you been waiting long?

9. Simple Future Tense

  • Subject + will + V1
  • Subject + will not + V1
  • Will + Subject + V1?
I will call you later.
She will not come.
Will you help me?

10. Future Continuous Tense

  • Subject + will be + V-ing
  • Subject + will not be + V-ing
  • Will + Subject + be + V-ing?
I will be waiting for you.
She will not be joining us.
Will they be staying overnight?

11. Future Perfect Tense

  • Subject + will have + V3
  • Subject + will not have + V3
  • Will + Subject + have + V3?
He will have finished the project.
They will not have left yet.
Will you have completed the test?

12. Future Perfect Continuous Tense

  • Subject + will have been + V-ing
  • Subject + will not have been + V-ing
  • Will + Subject + have been + V-ing?
She will have been working here for 5 years.
He will not have been living there.
Will you have been studying all night?
